GHB Prime Rib

Recipe for yums

The outside is colored like deep caramel, while the edges have a gentle crust. The outside contrasts well with the inside of the roast, with its fat softened and rendered, leaving the meat moist and melting.

Ingredients

  • prime rib
  • salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Rare: Target temp 115 deg F
  2. Medium Rare: Target temp 120 deg F
